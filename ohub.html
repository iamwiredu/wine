Internships

Optimize your chances of securing valuable internships in engineering and tech fields.

Where to Find Openings

  • Regularly check websites like LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and company-specific career pages
  • Set up job alerts for engineering and tech internships
  • Stay alert for university career services that provide lists of internship opportunities
  • Attend university and industry career fairs to meet recruiters
  • Directly check career or internship pages of companies you're interested in

Tips to Secure Engineering Internships

  • Begin your search months in advance as many companies recruit interns early
  • Tailor your resume/CV and cover letter towards relevant courses, projects, and technical skills
  • Customize your cover letter for each application, expressing specific interest in the company
  • Expand your network by reaching out to professors, alumni, and industry professionals
  • Prepare for interviews by researching common questions and understanding company values
  • Be flexible and consider various internships in your field to broaden experience
  • Build your skills: learn relevant software, programming languages, and participate in engineering clubs
  • Send a thank you email after interviews
