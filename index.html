Who We Are

Women in Engineering (WinE) KNUST

We are a student-led association at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology committed to inspiring and equipping female engineering students for leadership and technical excellence.

As a student chapter of Women in Engineering Ghana and a recognized group under the Ghana Engineering Students Association (GESA), we work to build confidence, create access to professional opportunities, and promote inclusion within STEM fields.

Our Key Programs

WinE Library

Explore key members, leadership insights, and success stories. Connect with national leaders, learn from inspiring journeys, and discover the achievements of women in renowned companies.

Main Events

Signature programs including WinE Akwaaba welcome events, WinE Connect Mentorship program, and She Can empowerment workshops focused on self-leadership and resilience.

Opportunity Hub

Connecting members to scholarships, internships, and strategic societies. We provide updates on local and international opportunities tailored to engineering students.

Health Education

Raising awareness on critical health topics including breast cancer, cervical cancer, menstrual hygiene, and mental health through seminars and campaigns.

Lifestyle Programs

Supporting balanced student life through professional branding workshops, LinkedIn optimization, fitness sessions, and friendly sports competitions.

WinE Blogs

Personal stories, interviews, and editorials through WinE Affair, plus weekly motivation and career tips through WinE Monday posts.
